About M.R.R.B Public School

M.R.R.B Public School, located in Kanchanpur, Deoria, offers a nurturing environment focused on holistic development. We blend academic excellence with modern facilities on one of the largest campuses in the region. Our learner-centric approach encourages not just learning but a lifelong love for it.

Established with a commitment to quality education, we provide a safe, clean, and stimulating environment where opportunities are created for every child to become truly educated, enriched, and empowered to innovate and create. We foster intellectual, moral, spiritual, and cultural growth.

Curriculum Highlights

We follow the Latest curriculum, providing a robust and recognized educational framework designed to foster critical thinking and comprehensive knowledge.

Academic Focus

  • Strong emphasis on core subjects: English, Hindi, Maths, Science, Social Studies.
  • Introduction to Computer Science and digital literacy from an early age.
  • Focus on language development and communication skills.
  • Well-equipped labs for practical Science learning.

Teaching Methodology

  • Activity-based and project-based learning to make concepts engaging.
  • Collaborative learning and group activities encouraged.
  • Focus on conceptual understanding over rote memorization.
  • Regular assessments and personalized feedback for improvement.

Co-curricular Activities

  • Sports: Football, Cricket, Badminton, Athletics, Indoor Games.
  • Arts & Crafts: Drawing, Painting, Craftwork.
  • Performing Arts: Music (Vocal/Instrumental), Dance.
  • Clubs and Competitions: Debate, Quiz, Science Club etc.

Our approach ensures students are not only academically proficient but also develop essential life skills, creativity, and a strong moral compass.

lund Criteria

As per government regulations, M.R.R.B Public School follows the mandatory age criteria for admissions and student APAAR ID generation.

  • Nursery: children should be 4 years old or older.
  • LKG: children should be 5 years old or older.
  • Class 1 onwards: Based on previous class completion & age criteria as per government rule.

Admission Process

  1. Obtain the Admission Form & Prospectus from the school office or download online.
  2. Submit the completed form along with required documents.
  3. Interaction/Assessment session (if applicable for the class).
  4. Notification of admission status.
  5. Completion of fee payment and formalities.

Required Documents

  • Completed Admission Form
  • Recent Passport-size Photographs (Student & Parents/Guardian)
  • Birth Certificate (Copy)
  • Previous School Report Card (for Class 1 onwards)
  • Transfer Certificate (Original, for Class 1 onwards)
  • Aadhaar Card (Copy - Student & Parents/Guardian)
  • [Any other specific document]
